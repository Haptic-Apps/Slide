Slide for Reddit

Slide is an open-source, ad-free Reddit browser for Android

keyboard_arrow_down

Beatiful

Slide has been crafted from Material Design principles, ensuring a smooth and beatiful experience tied in with Android.

Customizable

Customize Slide to your heart's content, with over 12,000 theme combinations.

Intuitive

Slide's flagship feature and namesake. All navigation is done through intuitive swipe gestures. Slide left for the next post, and slide right to go back.

Powerful

With features such as Read Later, user coloring/tagging, AMOLED black and red shift themes, and Gallery mode, Slide has even more features than the desktop Reddit web app.

Internals

Slide is based around the JRAW API wrapper for Java. Feel free to fork the repo and add features into the app! Send me a message and I'll look into merging it into the source!
